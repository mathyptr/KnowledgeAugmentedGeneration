KNOWLEDGE GRAPH STRUCTURE AND REASONING RULES

1. ENTITY DEFINITIONS
- WaterbaseWISE.WaterBody: Represents waterbody  ('waterBodyIdentifier' property). 
- WaterbaseWISE.ObservedPropertyDeterminand: Represents the observed property (e.g. Ammonium). Contains the biochemical properties inside the waterbody.
- WaterbaseWISE.AggregatedDataByWaterBody: Represents the aggregated data of waterbody. Each AggregatedData is linked to an waterBody via the 'waterBodyIdentifier' relationship.

2. RELATIONSHIP LOGIC (MULTI-HOP REASONING)
The system must navigate the relationships between nodes to answer:
- Interactions: To find interactions between WaterBody and observed property, identify the respective biological element (observed property) via the 'contains' relationship and check for the presence of interaction relationships between them.

3. BIOLOGICAL REASONING
For biological explanation, the system must extract information from the 'biology', 'mechanism', or 'description' properties associated with the WaterBody nodes or their relationships.
