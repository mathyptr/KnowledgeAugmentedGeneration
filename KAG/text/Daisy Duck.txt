Daisy Duck is a white duck with white plumage, a short orange beak, and webbed feet.
She has long eyelashes and the large bow typical of Disney heroines, a blouse with puffed sleeves, lace panties, large high heels, and sometimes a bracelet on her wrist. Daisy Duck is Donald Duck's girlfriend.

Little is known about Daisy Duck's family: daughter of Pa Duck and Ma Duck, she has a sister, Matilda, mother of Emy, Ely, and Evy, who lives in Swan Village.
